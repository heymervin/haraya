About This Comparison

The Fiesta concept celebrates the vibrant, joyful energy of Filipino celebrations with a bold color palette inspired by fiestas. This mockup explores 5 typography pairings, each featuring Jost (the current build font) in a different role.

Each pairing shows: the everaftr wordmark, a hero heading, body text, button styles, and a sample vendor card. All fonts are from Google Fonts (free, web-safe).

How to use this: Compare side-by-side on desktop, or scroll vertically on mobile. Look for readability, personality, and alignment with the Fiesta concept's celebratory energy.

Typography Recommendations for Fiesta

1st
Jost + Fraunces

Captures the celebratory, warm spirit of Filipino fiestas. Elegant enough for weddings, playful enough to feel joyful. The variable font brings flexibility and Fraunces' personality pairs beautifully with the vibrant Fiesta palette.

2nd
Jost Solo (Variable Weight)

The most distinctive option. Let the Fiesta colors and banderita accents be the personality while Jost provides clean, modern structure. This is the minimalist, tech-forward approach that feels fresh and contemporary.

3rd
Jost + Cormorant Garamond

The current build — safe and elegant. If you don't want to change fonts, this pairing works well. Just lean into bolder use of the Fiesta colors to add the celebratory energy. The "don't fix what isn't broken" option.
