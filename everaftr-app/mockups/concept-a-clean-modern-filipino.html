Design Philosophy

Apple's restraint meets Filipino craftsmanship. This design strips away visual noise to let Filipino cultural elements land with impact. White becomes an active design choice, not a neutral filler. The ocean-inspired teal connects to the Philippines' archipelago identity. The Thread preserves the weaving heritage metaphor in a single, refined element.

Cultural depth is expressed through language (ninongs, Pre-Cana, CENOMAR, lechon), not decoration. The result: a planning tool that feels modern, welcoming, and unmistakably Filipino.
